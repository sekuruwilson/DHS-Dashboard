Why do these locations exist?

Rwanda's demographic and health survey reports national-level averages (like "Rwanda/National") and regional-level averages (like "Eastern Province"). To store these aggregate values in our structured database without breaking relationships, they are modeled as unique "District" targets. They should not be counted as actual geographical districts, but they are crucial for regional charts and national summary statistics.
